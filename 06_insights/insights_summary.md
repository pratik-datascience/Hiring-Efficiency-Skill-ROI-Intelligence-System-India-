# Key Insights – Hiring Efficiency & Skill-ROI Analysis

1.  Python and SQL deliver the highest Skill ROI, achieving stronger performance ratings relative to salary compared to other skills in the dataset.
    
2.  Even with single-skill employees, noticeable performance differences exist across skills, indicating that skill selection plays a critical role in hiring efficiency.
    
3.  IT companies exhibit lower attrition rates than manufacturing and non-IT firms, suggesting better employee retention strategies and role alignment.
    
4.  Salary is not a reliable predictor of performance—several mid-salary employees outperform higher-paid peers, highlighting inefficiencies in compensation structures.
    
5.  New hires in metro cities demonstrate faster early-stage performance growth**, particularly within the first 12 months of employment.
